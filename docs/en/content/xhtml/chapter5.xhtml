5. Sources of Information

Information can be gathered from a range of different sources.

These can be divided into categories:

Open Source:
  • TV/Radio
  • Print media
  • Internet
  • Social Media
Workers and General Public:
  • Fishermen
  • Construction workers
  • Street vendors
Service Providers:
  • Taxi drivers
  • Bus operators
  • Social workers
  • Shipping agents
  • NGOs
Criminal Sources:
  • Informants
  • Suspects
Official Sources:
  • Officers from your agency
  • Other government agencies
  • International agencies
  • Neighboring Border Liaison Offices
